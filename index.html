Book One ECLIPTIA'S
DAWN The Rise of the Prem Rath

In a future where Earth has collapsed and humanity struggles to rebuild on the distant planet of Ecliptia — a powerful narrative of rebellion, love, and cosmic destiny unfolds.

A diverse group of outcasts — led by the enigmatic Joran — battle the oppressive Guardians of the Status Quo (G.O.S.Q.) and search for the legendary Prem Rath: a mystical chariot of love holding the key to restoring balance in a fractured multiverse.
